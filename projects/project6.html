Project Description

Timmed mobile is a social network management tool that helps you maintain a unique online character across numerous social networks. How does it work? 1. Compose your unique post (text or picture) 2. Schedule time for post (or post immediately) 3. Schedule delete time for post (if you don’t want post to be permanent) 4. Select social network you want to make post 5. Schedule Post Extra: Did you change your mind about a “scheduled” post? Fear not, we got you covered. All scheduled posts can be edited and rescheduled. Timmed uses a simple interface to keep track of your feeds, selected social network and deleted posts. We let you manage your future staying in the present
